Shirts

PyCon Australia uses Gildan t-shirts, and this year's design will be available in a variety of colours that you can choose from on the day. There are three varieties available:

  • The Men's/Straight-Cut option is the Ultra Cotton "Classic Fit" shirt
  • The Women's Semi-Fitted option is the Heavy Cotton Semi-Fitted Ladies' shirt
  • The Women's Classic Fit option is the Ultra Cotton "Classic Fit" Ladies' shirt
